COLUMN REORDERING + CAPACITY VALIDATION
========================================

CHANGE 1: Columns Reordered
----------------------------

**NEW ORDER:**
┌──────────────┬──────────┬───────────┬──────────┬──────────┬──────────────┬────────┐
│ Room Type    │ Arrivals │ Available │ Due Outs │ In House │ Overbooked By│ Status │
├──────────────┼──────────┼───────────┼──────────┼──────────┼──────────────┼────────┤
│ KNGN         │    91    │    21     │    12    │    85    │      -       │   OK   │
│ TDBN         │   119    │    31     │    15    │   110    │      -       │   OK   │
└──────────────┴──────────┴───────────┴──────────┴──────────┴──────────────┴────────┘

**Logic Flow (Left to Right):**
1. **Arrivals** - How many guests checking in today
2. **Available** - How many rooms available for those arrivals
3. **Due Outs** - How many leaving today (freeing up rooms)
4. **In House** - How many staying over from yesterday
5. **Overbooked By** - How many over capacity
6. **Status** - OVERBOOKED or OK


CHANGE 2: 321 Room Capacity Validation
---------------------------------------

**Constraint:** (In House - Due Outs) + Arrivals ≤ 321

**Formula Breakdown:**
- In House: 200 guests staying over
- Due Outs: 50 checking out today
- Net staying: 200 - 50 = 150
- Arrivals: 180 checking in today
- **Total Occupancy: 150 + 180 = 330 rooms** ⚠️

**If Total > 321:**
An orange warning row appears below the totals:

┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│ ⚠️ CAPACITY ALERT: Net Occupancy = 330 rooms (9 over capacity)              │
│ Formula: (In House 200 - Due Outs 50) + Arrivals 180 = 330                 │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

**Automatic Correction:**
The system automatically reduces "In House" counts proportionally to bring 
total occupancy down to 321 or below. This ensures realistic scenarios.

Why This Matters:
-----------------

1. **Column Order Makes Sense**
   - Left to right follows the MOD's thought process
   - "I have X arrivals, Y rooms available, Z leaving, W staying"

2. **Capacity Validation Prevents Errors**
   - Can't accidentally create scenarios with 400 occupied rooms
   - Realistic hotel operations
   - Clear warning when data doesn't make sense

3. **Professional Presentation**
   - Matches industry standard reporting
   - Easy to scan and understand
   - Totals at bottom with validation
